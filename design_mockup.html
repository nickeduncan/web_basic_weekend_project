Seeing is Believing

"Sibme is a video coaching and collaboration platform designed to improve teaching and learning in schools, insititutions, and education-related organizations."

How does Sibme improve teaching and learning?

"Sibme enhances classroom observation, allowing teachers and instructional leader to record classroom instruction for th epurpose of reflection, conversation, and feedback."

Observe

Watch yourself and others teach anytime, anywhere, and form any device.

Reflect

Access your private Workspace to reflect and annotate on your teaching and give and receive time-stamp feedback with trusted colleagues in shared spaces we call Huddles.

Improve

Purposefully interact with video content to improve instruction and student outcomes.
